## About This Project

FraudLens AI is a real-time fraud detection and decision intelligence system built for small and mid-sized banks. It analyzes live and historical transaction data to detect suspicious behavior, generate risk scores, and provide clear, human-readable explanations for every flagged transaction.

Unlike traditional black-box fraud systems, Risk Investigators AI combines machine learning with LLM-powered reasoning to act as a "fraud investigator," helping banking teams quickly understand, trust, and act on risky transactions. It enables instant approve/block decisions through an integrated workflow, making enterprise-grade fraud protection accessible and affordable for smaller financial institutions.
