## About This Project

Two people on our team were in a car accident. The insurance company called within 48 hours with a settlement offer. They had a script refined over decades. Our teammates had nothing. That's the problem we're solving today.

Parity is an AI-powered claims companion that gives insurance claimants real-time guidance during calls from at-fault insurers. It levels the playing field between individual claimants (who have no experience with claims), and insurance adjusters who are professionally trained to minimise payouts.
