## About This Project

SignalForge AI is an AI-powered trading agent platform that lets anyone create personalized market analysis agents in natural language, without writing code.

Instead of relying on generic bots, users can describe their own strategy in plain English — for example, "momentum scalper on XAUUSD, 15-minute timeframe, trigger on RSI divergence and volume spike" — and SignalForge turns that into a disciplined, structured AI agent running 24/7 across stocks, crypto, forex, commodities, and ETFs.

### Key Features

- **Agent Builder** for creating custom trading agents with natural language instructions, indicators, and trigger conditions
- **Structured Signal Cards** with precise entry, target, stop-loss, confidence score, and risk/reward ratio
- **Real-time Signal Feed** showing recent AI-generated opportunities across multiple assets
- **Multi-asset support** across crypto, equities, forex, commodities, and ETFs
- **Alerting** via push, email, and Telegram
- **Community publishing layer** for sharing high-conviction ideas

SignalForge is designed to bridge deep financial research and usable product workflows, making institutional-style analysis accessible through a simple agent interface.
