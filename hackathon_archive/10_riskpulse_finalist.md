## About This Project

RiskPulse is an AI-driven underwriting engine that transforms static financial documents into a living "pulse" of borrower health. By replacing manual data entry with high-precision extraction and dynamic risk modeling, RiskPulse allows credit officers to diagnose financial stability in seconds.

### Why RiskPulse Stands Out

- **Intelligent Diagnostics:** Using advanced OCR, RiskPulse extracts key financial data from complex 10-K and 10-Q filings, automatically calculating 12+ critical ratios across Liquidity, Leverage, Coverage, and Profitability.

- **Calibrated Risk Scoring:** Unlike one-size-fits-all tools, RiskPulse applies a Weighted Scoring Algorithm tailored to the specific industry (e.g., Technology), geography (e.g., U.S. Tier 1), and business stage of the borrower.

- **Automatic Validation Halt:** To prevent flawed underwriting, the system features a built-in "Safety Trigger" — halting calculations and flagging Data Gaps if essential inputs like Total Assets or Operating Cash Flow are missing.

- **The Auto-Credit Memo:** RiskPulse synthesizes its findings into a professional, institutional-grade Credit Memo, complete with an executive summary, risk drivers, and an automated "Approve/Decline" status based on calibrated policy limits.
